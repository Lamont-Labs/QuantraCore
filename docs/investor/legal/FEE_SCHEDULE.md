## 5. Billing Terms

### 5.1 Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (US only)
- Wire transfer (institutional only)
- Cryptocurrency (select coins, contact sales)

### 5.2 Billing Cycle
- Monthly: Charged on subscription date each month
- Annual: Charged in full at start of period

### 5.3 Refunds
- Monthly subscriptions: No refunds for partial months
- Annual subscriptions: Pro-rata refund within first 30 days
- Add-ons: No refunds once activated

### 5.4 Price Changes
- 30 days notice for price increases
- Existing subscribers locked in for current term

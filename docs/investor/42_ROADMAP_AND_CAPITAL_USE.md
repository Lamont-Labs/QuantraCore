## 12-18 Month Roadmap

**Focus:** Team building and infrastructure

| Milestone | Deliverable |

| Data feeds | Premium data subscriptions |

- 2 engineers productive

**Exit Criteria:**

## Capital Allocation

### Engineering (50-60%)

| Line Item | % of Category | Purpose |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Salaries (2-3 engineers) | 80% | Core development team |
| Contractors | 10% | Specialized work |
| Tools/licenses | 10% | Development tools |

**Rationale:** Engineering is the critical path. Quality engineers are the primary need.

### Infrastructure (15-20%)

| Line Item | % of Category | Purpose |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Cloud hosting | 40% | Production infrastructure |
| Data feeds | 40% | Premium market data |
| Dev/test environments | 20% | Non-production systems |

**Rationale:** Production-grade infrastructure and quality data are essential for credibility.

### Compliance/Legal (10-15%)

| Line Item | % of Category | Purpose |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Legal counsel | 50% | Contracts, IP protection |
| Compliance review | 30% | Regulatory assessment |
| IP protection | 20% | Patents, trademarks |

**Rationale:** Institutional customers require proper legal and compliance posture.

### Founder Runway (10-15%)

| Line Item | % of Category | Purpose |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Founder salary | 80% | Full-time focus |
| Travel | 10% | Customer meetings |
| Contingency | 10% | Unexpected needs |

**Rationale:** Founder needs runway to focus full-time on architecture and direction.
